FAQ'S

Does each individual member in team events need a separate ticket?

No, only one 1 ticket needed for a team

Does participants coming for Informal or general events only, be issued an Asthra Pass?

NO ,asthra pass is only for joining in dept events

Should every college id be verified?

Yes

If someone has already registered for asthra pass and wishes to join any workshop or vice versa be issued a different card?

Yes and both types of passes have different costs and hence discretion is necessary

Should I Know everything about the events in my department?

Absolutely yes.

What to do if someone already paid online but the payment verification is unsuccessful?

Send them to the startup boot camp (makers space) inside the copy shop

What to do if they can't provide any ID card?

Tell them to show any proof even if it's digital like aadhar, license etc.

What to do if someone lost their card/receipt issued from the front desk?

First ensure that a card had been issued then you can use their asthra pass or workshop pass and register them for events.

What if a mistake was made while entering data?

A confirmation page is provided after every submission at the front desk, volunteers are required to ensure that the data entered is proper whilst typing as well as after and contact the web team if any such error was made. Fields like phone number, name and email id are absolutely inevitably crucial as failure to upload correct information may deny them their certificates
